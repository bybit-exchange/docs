### HTTP 請求
GET `/v5/earn/position`

### 請求參數
| 參數 | 是否必需 | 類型 | 說明|
|:----- |:-------|:-----|------ |
|category|**true**|string|產品類別：`FlexibleSaving`,`OnChain`|
|productId|false|string|持倉對應的產品 ID|
|coin|false|string|幣種名稱|

### 響應參數
| 參數 | 類型 | 說明|
|:----- |:-----|----- |
|list|array|Object|
|> coin|string |幣種名稱 |
|> productId|string |持倉對應的產品 ID|
|> amount|string |持倉金額|
|> totalPnl|string |持倉總收益。僅在 Onchain 非 LST 模式下有價值|
|> claimableYield|string |Yield accrues on an hourly basis and is distributed at 00:30 UTC daily. If you unstake your assets before yield distribution, any undistributed yield will be credited to your account along with your principal.|
|> id|string |持倉ID. Only for Onchain|
|> status|string |`Processing`,`Active`. Only for Onchain|
|> orderId|string |Order Id. Only for Onchain|
|> estimateRedeemTime|string |Estimate redeem time. Only for Onchain|
|> estimateStakeTime|string |Estimate stake time. Only for Onchain|
|> estimateInterestCalculationTime|string |Estimated Interest accrual time. Only for Onchain |
|> settlementTime|string |Settlement time. Only has value for Onchain `Fixed` product|
