### accountType
* `UNIFIED`&emsp;統一帳戶
* `CONTRACT`&emsp;期貨帳戶
* `COPYTRADING`&emsp;跟單帳戶

### symbol

> 跟單交易僅支持USDT永續

* `BTCUSDT`
* `ETHUSDT`
* `SOLUSDT`
* `...`

### orderType
* `Market`
* `Limit`

### triggerBy
* `LastPrice`
* `IndexPrice`
* `MarkPrice`

### copyTradeOrderType
* `ACTIVE`
* `TPSLCONDITIONAL`
* `OPENFILLED`

### timeInForce
* `GoodTillCancel`
* `ImmediateOrCancel`
* `FillOrKill`
* `PostOnly`

### positionIdx
* `0`&emsp;單向持倉
* `1`&emsp;買側的雙向持倉
* `2`&emsp;賣側的雙向持倉

### interval
* `1` `3` `5` `15` `30` `60` `120` `240` `360` `720`&emsp;分鐘
* `D`&emsp;天
* `W`&emsp;週
* `M`&emsp;月

### tickDirection
* `PlusTick`&emsp;價格上漲
* `ZeroPlusTick`&emsp;交易的價格與前一筆交易的價格相同，前一筆交易的價格高於前一筆交易的價格
* `MinusTick`&emsp;價格下跌
* `ZeroMinusTick`&emsp;交易的價格與前一筆交易的價格相同，前一筆交易的價格低於前一筆交易的價格
