**Topic:**<br/>
`copyTradeOrder`

### Response Parameters
| Parameter | Type | Comments|
|:----- |:-----|----- |
|orderId |string |Order Id |
|orderLinkId |string |Unique user-set order ID. Maximum length of 36 characters |
|symbol |string |Symbol |
|[orderType](../enum#ordertype) |string |Conditional order type |
|price |string |Order price |
|qty |string |Transaction qty |
|side |string |Side  |
|[positionIdx](../enum#positionidx) |string |Position idx, used to identify positions in different position modes: 1-Buy side of both side mode; 2-Sell side of both side mode |
|cumExecFee |string |Cumulative trading fees |
|cumExecValue |string |Cumulative value of trading |
|lastExecPrice |string |Last execution price |
|cumExecQty |string |Cumulative qty of trading |
|takeProfit |string |Take profit price |
|stopLoss |string |Stop loss price |
|[tpTriggerBy](../enum#triggerby) |string |Type of take-profit activation price, LastPrice by default. |
|[slTriggerBy](../enum#triggerby) |string |Type of stop-loss activation price, LastPrice by default. |
|createTime |number |Commission time |
|updateTime |number |Update time |
|[copyTradeOrderStatus](../enum#copytradeorderstatus) |string |Copy trade order status |

### Request Example

```http
ws.send('{"op": "subscribe", "args": ["copyTradeOrder"]}')
```
