### HTTP Request
POST `/v5/p2p/item/create`

### Request Parameters
| Parameter | Required | Type | Comments|
|:----- |:-------|:-----|------ |
|tokenId|<b>true</b> |string |token id. e.g. USDT,ETH,BTC |
|currencyId|<b>true</b> |string |currency id e.g HKD,USD,EUR |
|side|<b>true</b> |string |ad for buying token or for selling token. 0 - buy; 1 - sell |
|priceType|<b>true</b> |string |Ads Pricing Exchange Rate Model. 0 - Fixed rate; 1 - Floating rate |
|premium|<b>true</b> |string |Floating ratio with current exchange rate |
|price|<b>true</b> |string |ads price |
|minAmount|<b>true</b> |string |Min transaction amount |
|maxAmount|<b>true</b> |string |Max transaction amount |
|remark|<b>true</b> |string |Transaction Description(max=900) |
|tradingPreferenceSet|<b>true</b> |Object |Trading Preferences |
|> hasUnPostAd |string | Whether the counterparty must not have posted any advertisements： 0：unneed，1：need |
|> isKyc |string | Is it necessary for the counterparty to complete identity authentication?： 0：unneed，1：need |
|> isEmail |string | Is it necessary for the counterparty to bind an email address?：0：unneed，1：need |
|> isMobile |string | Is it necessary for the counterparty to bind an telphone?：0：unneed，1：need |
|> hasRegisterTime |string | Is the registration time required to be no less than {} days?0：unneed，1：need |
|> registerTimeThreshold |string | Registration time threshold（Unit: Day）|
|> orderFinishNumberDay30 |string | Limit on number of completed orders in the last 30 days |
|> completeRateDay30 |string | Completion rate limit in the last 30 days |
|> nationalLimit |string | Restricted KYC Countries;IOS 3 CODE |
|> hasOrderFinishNumberDay30 |string | Is it necessary to have no less than {} orders in the last 30 days? 0：unneed，1：need |
|> hasCompleteRateDay30 |string | Is it necessary to have a completion rate of no less than {} in the last 30 days?：0：unneed，1：need |
|> hasNationalLimit |string | Are country restrictions required;0：unneed，1：need |
|paymentIds|<b>true</b> |array[string] |Payment method type id(len<=5) |
|quantity|<b>true</b> |string |Number of ads |
|price|<b>true</b> |string |ads price |
|paymentPeriod|<b>true</b> |string |payment duration |
|itemType|<b>true</b> |string |ORIGIN - original P2P advertisement, generally refers to non-bulk advertisement; BULK - bulk advertisement |


### Response Parameters
| Parameter | Type | Comments|
|:----- |:-----|----- |
|[accountType](../enum#accounttype) |string |Account type|
|memberId |string |UserID|
|balance |array |Object |
|> coin |string |Currency|
|> walletBalance |string |Wallet balance|
|> transferBalance |string |Transferable balance|
|> bonus |string |Bonus|
